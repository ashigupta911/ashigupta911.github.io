Our Menu

Unexpected flavors forged from nature and mingled with flourish. Vibrant herbs grown as close to home as it gets (they’re from the rooftop garden). The freshest ingredients responsibly sourced both near and far.

Chicken



This is a pretty chicken stew recipe, loaded with tender chicken, red potatoes, carrots, celery, onion, garlic, tomatoes, and one ingredient that gives it it’s signature flavor. The garlic here is an absolute must. Made with olive oil and has a base flavour of tomato puree. this dish will make you fall in love.

Beef



Succulent roast topside of beef, made especially irresistible with a mustard crust, piquant shallots and red wine enriched gravy.Silverside and topside are cheaper and leaner cuts of meat, well-suited to slow-cooking. The stock and juices make a great spicy sauce when combined with caramelised onions.

Sushi



Sushi is a Japanese dish of prepared vinegared rice, usually with some sugar and salt, accompanying a variety of ingredients,such as seafood, vegetables, and occasionally tropical fruits. The wasabi comes on the sushi and includes diced or shredded tuna with hot peppers.This dish is a must have.
